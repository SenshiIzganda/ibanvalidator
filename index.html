🏦 IBAN Validator

An IBAN (International Bank Account Number) is a standardised way of identifying bank accounts across national borders. It was originally adopted by the European Committee for Banking Standards (ECBS) and later as an international standard under ISO 13616.

How Validation Works

  • Check country code exists
  • Verify correct IBAN length
  • Alphanumeric characters only
  • MOD-97 check digit verification
  • Extract BBAN components

Supported Regions

  • Europe (all countries)
  • Middle East & North Africa
  • Caribbean & Latin America
  • Sub-Saharan Africa
  • Central Asia
